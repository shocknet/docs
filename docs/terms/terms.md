## Privacy

We do not deal in fiat shitcoins and therefore do not collect any KYC or PII data typically associated with those things.
Even email addresses are optional.

Also,

## Fees

Fees vary from service to service and are based on market conditions for Bitcoin relative to datacenter resources.
We provide bandwidth, storage, compute, name-space and other cloud services frictionlessly to users of otherwise decentralized protocols. These services are accessed granularly, using micro-payments over Bitcoin’s Lightning Network.

As a hosting provider, we do not keep a percentage of monetization revenues on content served from our infrastructure.

Self-custodial monetization tools are sustained through charges for relay access or bootstrap peering.
Withdrawals on excess service balances, swaps, bootstrap peering, etc are subject to network enforced routing or chain fees.
